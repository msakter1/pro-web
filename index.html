About Me

Hi, I'm Musmmat Akter, a junior studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan with a passion for web development and educational technology. My journey in tech has taken me across borders—studying machine learning at Yonsei University—broadening my perspective on global education and innovation.

I have hands-on experience in C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, and I enjoy building interactive applications that enhance learning experiences. My ultimate goal is to make STEM education more accessible, especially for girls in underserved communities.

Currently, I'm tutoring a young Afghan girl in math. Since she can’t attend school, I’ve had to come up with creative ways to make learning engaging and effective. This experience has fueled my desire to develop an interactive platform that makes learning math and coding easier for girls in impoverished areas.

Beyond this personal mission, I aspire to work as a web developer, building scalable and user-friendly applications.

If you're interested in collaborating or learning more about my work, feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn!
